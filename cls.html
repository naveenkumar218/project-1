Cookies: Clicking "Set Cookie" will store a cookie named username. "Get Cookie" will display all cookies for the domain, and "Delete Cookie" removes the username cookie. Local Storage: Clicking "Set Local Storage" stores username in Local Storage. "Get Local Storage" retrieves it, and "Clear Local Storage" removes it. Session Storage: Clicking "Set Session Storage" stores username in Session Storage for the session duration. "Get Session Storage" retrieves it, and "Clear Session Storage" removes it.
